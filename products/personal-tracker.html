Frequently Asked Questions

How is this different from budgeting apps?

No monthly subscription. No bank account syncing (more privacy). No app to download. Just a simple spreadsheet you own forever. You enter expenses manually, which actually helps you be more mindful of spending.

Do I have to enter every single expense?

For best results, yes. But even tracking 80% of expenses gives you valuable insights. Start with the big categories (food, bills, transport) and add more detail over time.

Can I use this for a couple or family?

Yes! You can either share one spreadsheet and both add expenses, or each person can have their own tracker. For shared expenses, just agree who logs what.

What if my categories are different?

The 13 categories cover most personal expenses, but you have full access to the spreadsheet. You can rename categories, add new ones, or merge them to fit your life.

How long does it take each day?

About 30 seconds per expense. Most people spend 2-5 minutes per day logging all their purchases. Many find it easiest to do it once at the end of each day.

Will this actually help me save money?

Awareness is the first step. When you see exactly where your money goes, you naturally start making better choices. Most users report finding "leaks" they didn't know existed.
